Maintenance & Service

The Maintenance & Service Unit provides comprehensive technical support for industrial plants, commercial buildings, and public facilities. We focus on preventive and corrective maintenance to ensure smooth and continuous operations.

Key services

  • Preventive and predictive maintenance programs
  • Breakdown maintenance and emergency repair services
  • Annual maintenance contracts (AMC)
  • Building services maintenance (water, HVAC, pumps, etc.)
  • Calibration and testing services
  • Spare parts management and replacement
  • Sustainability-focused maintenance to reduce energy waste

Our approach

We use modern diagnostic tools, smart monitoring systems, and skilled technical teams to minimize downtime and extend equipment life while improving operational efficiency.

Electrical & Mechanical

The Electrical & Mechanical Engineering Unit delivers design, installation, upgrading, and maintenance solutions across a wide range of electrical and mechanical systems. We support industrial, commercial, and institutional clients with safe, efficient, and compliant engineering services.

Key services

  • Electrical installation and wiring systems
  • Power distribution, switchgear, and control panels
  • Generator, motor, and pump installation & maintenance
  • HVAC, ventilation, and refrigeration systems
  • Mechanical fabrication and structural works
  • Equipment commissioning and performance testing
  • Energy-efficient system upgrades and retrofits

Our specialty

Agricultural Development & Farm Management

This unit focuses on improving agricultural productivity through scientific farm planning, climate-smart agriculture, and sustainable land management practices. We support small-scale and large-scale farms from planning to harvesting.

Key services

  • Farm planning and crop selection
  • Organic and sustainable agriculture practices
  • Irrigation and water-management solutions
  • Soil testing and fertility management
  • Greenhouse and protected cultivation systems
  • Precision agriculture and digital monitoring
  • Training and advisory services for farmers

Our focus

Agro-Engineering & Farm Mechanization

The Agro-Engineering & Farm Mechanization Unit integrates engineering technology into agriculture to reduce labor, increase efficiency, and support large-scale operations.

Key services

  • Selection and supply of agricultural machinery and equipment
  • Tractor, harvester, and implement service & maintenance
  • Design of irrigation systems (drip, sprinkler, solar-powered)
  • Development of greenhouse and hydroponic systems
  • Post-harvest handling & storage systems
  • Farm structure design (sheds, storage, drying yards)
  • Renewable-energy solutions for agriculture

Our approach
